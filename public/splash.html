BATTLE SPACE SHIPS

Hit LAUNCH to play!

Poke to mute

How to play:


Number of players: 2


Objective: Locate and destroy your opponents spaceships.


Set up:


  • Each player has 5 spaceships:
    (2 Saucers, 2 Destroyers and 1 Freighter)

  • Each player places spaceships within the grid
    representing their galactic quadrant.

  • Space ships may either be placed vertically or
    horizontally and cannot be moved in game!

Rules:

  1. Players take it in turns firing lasers at chosen
    coordinates of the opponents quadrant.

  2. The lasers fired will either hit or miss.

  3. Animated icons will indicate this hit or miss.
